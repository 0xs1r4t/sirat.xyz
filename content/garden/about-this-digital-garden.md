# personal (temporary) philosophy

sometimes you gotta say _“fuck ai”_ and do things by yourself. such as these wonderful icons. i made them with my bare hands. the same can’t be said for this code. claude happens to be an awfully helpful and selfless pair programmer. thnx claude ♡

i made the icons with many references and some weird and convoluted techniques because i’ve never made pixel art before. i might document them soon, even though they don’t have much to do with computer science, except for the part where i converted them into svgs. many thanks to the [Pixel.svg](https://codepen.io/shshaw/full/XbxvNj) tool, created by [Shaw](https://codepen.io/shshaw), that i found on codepen for the same. i like making pixel art now, so more of it will be visible here soon.

# current goal

this 2025, i hope the world works on ai models of different kinds that help us do the stuff we don’t want to do, rather than taking the joy out of tasks we use to unwind 🤓👍
